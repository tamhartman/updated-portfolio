Philosophy

Take everything in - good, bad, beautiful, ugly. Find inspiration in all of it. Transmute the tactile wonder of the physical world into an experience that can be held in something as small as the palm of your hand, yet feels large enough to saturate the whole body. Revel in acts of spontaneous beauty. Create things that you truly, deeply, believe in. Be flexible, learn, venture into the unknown. Explore always, and most of all, build something awesome.

About me

Immersive Experiences. UX. Games. Prototyping

Hi. I'm Tam :)

Tamara Hartman is a game designer, UX/UI designer, and digital artist with a background in immersive experiences and rapid prototyping. Well versed in a variety of game engines, design and multimedia software, and programming languages, Tamara is concerned with creating engaging, beautifully designed experiences across a multitude of platforms and formats. Originally from South Florida, Tamara holds degrees from California Institute of the Arts, NYU, and the University of Miami. She currently resides in New York with her dog, Mochi, where she is an avid snowboarder, geekery generalist, and coffee drinker.
